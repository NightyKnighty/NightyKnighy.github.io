This morning, i spent 2 hours trying to get the footer to stick to the bottom because there was a bug that drag the content area along with the footer, resulting a very ugly website. What a waste of time... BUT, i still learnt a very important debugging lesson which is that the margin boundaries along the same plane can affect the appearance of the website when it is zoomed.

A few hours later, i thought of adding a seach bar in the website. I came across Google Custom Seach and started to work on it as shown on the right of the website.
