A typical Sunday before i go back to NS

This morning, i spent 2 hours trying to get the footer to stick to the bottom because there was a bug that drag the content area along with the footer, resulting a very ugly website. I later found out that it was not the footer's problem but the content on the left and right which overlaped with the content in the centre, pushing the centre content downwards. What a waste of time... BUT, i still learnt a very important debugging lesson which is that the margin boundaries along the same plane can affect the appearance of the website when it is zoomed.

A few hours later, i thought of adding a seach bar in the website. I came across Google Custom Seach and started to work on it as shown on the right of the website. It was quite a challenge cause i am completely new to this, but if you search the internet long enough, you will be able to find the answers to your questions. in the process of finding the answers to my questions, i stubble upon two helpful websites that gave me alot of advice in coding html. They are stackover flow and Css Tricks. They have been my source of help whenever a new idea pop into my head for my website.
